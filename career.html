    Perks and Benefits!!!

    Clavrit offers benefits in order to encourage a culture of joy & self-accomplishment by catering to individual needs with respect to flexibility, career enhancement and an enriched personal life. Our goal is to make Clavrit an employee-friendly company, and we strive hard to maintain transparency, diversity, and equal opportunities for everyone.

    Flexible Work Hour

    Clavrit values productivity at work and understands that it comes naturally with a balanced personal life. Hence we ensure that the employees can fulfill their family commitments, allowing them flexibility when it comes to choosing work hours wherever possible, so an enriched personal and work-life balance is maintained.

    Culture

    At clavrit, we strive to ensure that everyone can retain their identity that reflects their cultural experiences and feelings. Where people can be who they are and be their best, professionally and personally.

    Work Life Balance

    Our employees enjoy a perfect work-life balance at Clavrit, we offer perks like birthday leaves, time off from work etc encouraging employees to relax & rejuvinate.

    Equal Opportunities

    Clavrit is an equal opportunities employer and welcomes applications from all sections of society and does not discriminate on grounds of race, religion or belief, ethnic or national origin, disability, age, citizenship, marital, domestic or civil partnership status, sexual orientation, gender identity. Our employees value the freedom of thought & transparency in communication & surplus opportunities to advance their careers.

    Stimulating Work Environment

    At Clavrit, you will have opportunities to hone your functional skills and expertise in different areas of specialization. The sheer variety of work we do, and the experience it offers, provide an unbeatable platform from which to build a career. We work hard to establish a welcoming workplace environment where employees walk in to experience new possibilities & challenges & feel encouraged to share their ideas & permitted to explore new horizons.

    Opportunities For Career

    As we aim for "uncompromised perfection" we strive for creativity & improvement in every project that we take. We at Clavrit truly understand the importance of training and keeping our employees updated with the latest technology and market trend. We make regular arrangement for training sessions & seminars for all our employees to learn and grow individually in their respective domain.

    We Care

    Our employees feel secure from their health perspective as each employee is covered under amazing Health benefits with group health insurance, fund lending support provided to employees as when required. Not to mention the "secret santa" support provided to local NGO's on regular bases by clavrit. We support our extended families (families of our employees) with vocational trainings & other skill development activities to support them and enable them to be self-dependent.

    Remote Work

    We have diverse options to choose from as to how you want to work: from onsite to Hybrid & remote working models we stand out as the only company to have offerred WFA i.e work from anywhere round the year even after covid.

    Appreciations, Reward & Recognition

    Appreciation is something which plays a vital role in boosting the self-confidence of every employee which is turn translates into the best performance from an individual. We at Clavrit, understand how important it is to recognize and reward employees for their efforts and dedication which they put in, going out of their comfort zone ! We strive to ensure that employees receive regular feedforward & recognition for their efforts. From lucrative incentives to Awards and certificates, We at Clavrit make sure to recognize stellar employees and reward them for their outstanding contribution towards the company's growth & success.
